The Toyota way

Let's Go Places

We’re in the business of making great cars and trucks. But we also work every day to apply and share our know-how in ways that benefit people, the community, and our planet in order to build a better tomorrow.

The way we work

How do we create some of the most advanced, reliable and safe vehicles? We’re always looking for ways to improve our operations, always challenging ourselves to innovate, and always improving each day in everything we do.

Our Purpose

We create vehicles by listening and responding to you. Why? Because it's our belief that our cars should do more than help you go places on the road, they should also help you go places in life.

What drives us forward

60 years in your service! Through the unique relationship with Toyota Motor Company, Abdul Latif Jameel has grown continuously over the years to become one of the leading entities in the automotive sector in the Kingdom.

The Sky is out Limit

In 1945 Abdul Latif Jameel started his business with a philosophy and vision to put his customers, or ‘Guests’, first. This underlying principle, coupled with Abdul Latif Jameel’s visionary approach, formed the foundations of the company that still bears his name today. Before, there were many paved roads, Abdul Latif Jameel saw the benefits of introducing 4x4 vehicles. His first order of four “BJ” model 4x4 vehicles was the start of a long-standing relationship with Toyota Motor Corporation.
